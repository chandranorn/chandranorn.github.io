About me

Chandra Norn | Graphic Designer + Illustrator

Chandra Norn is a Communication Designer and Illustrator based in Vancouver, BC. Her experiences growing up in a small mountain town surrounded by roaming wildlife and rushing rivers inspired her love for art and design. Being raised by a family of artists helped her learn the basic technical skills of drawing, painting and using design software, but it wasnâ€™t until she attended post-secondary that her professional life as an artist truly began.

She graduated with distinction from the Communication Design Essentials program in 2020 and looks forward to breathing life into her clients visions using her newly accomplished skills.
